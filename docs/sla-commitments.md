# SLA Commitments

## Status: To Be Defined

CSA is in the process of defining formal service level agreements (SLAs) for vulnerability response times. This document will be updated as those commitments are established.

## Current Approach

While formal SLAs are being defined, CSA's approach is:

- **Acknowledge reports promptly**
- **Publish advisories openly and quickly** — public disclosure is the default
- **Prioritize by severity** — critical and high severity issues are addressed first
- **Communicate with reporters** — keep reporters informed through the advisory comment thread

## Why SLAs Are Not Yet Defined

CSA is a nonprofit with limited resources. Rather than commit to SLAs we cannot reliably meet, we are establishing the program first and defining commitments based on actual operational experience.

This document will be updated when SLAs are formalized.
